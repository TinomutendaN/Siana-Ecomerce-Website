About Masho

Crafting timeless leather goods with integrity and care.

Our Story

Mashiri was founded with a simple mission: to create high-quality leather goods that stand the test of time. We believe in the power of simplicity, functionality, and exceptional craftsmanship. Our products are made from the finest materials, carefully selected to ensure durability and longevity.

We started with a passion for leather and a desire to create something that would last. Today, WP Standard is known for its commitment to quality, with each piece designed to age beautifully and tell its own unique story. We are proud of the products we create and the relationships we build with our customers, who share our appreciation for timeless design and craftsmanship.

Our Philosophy

At MASHIRI, we believe that less is more. We focus on creating products that are functional, durable, and beautiful in their simplicity. Our design process is centered around the idea that every product should serve a purpose and be built to last.

We are committed to ethical practices in every step of our process, from sourcing materials to manufacturing. Our leather is sourced from reputable tanneries that prioritize sustainability and environmental responsibility. We work with skilled artisans who share our dedication to quality, ensuring that each product is made with care and attention to detail.
